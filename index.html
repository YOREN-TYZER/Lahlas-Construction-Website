Who We Are

Your Trusted
Construction Partner

Lahlas Construction is a 100% Black Economic Empowerment construction company founded in 2024 in Kromhoek village, Limpopo. Led by founder and Managing Director Mr. Lotus Nkhwashu, we were born from a clear mission: to fight unemployment, reduce poverty, and drive real economic development in rural South Africa.

Rooted in the principles of Batho Pele — placing people first — we deliver reliable, high-quality workmanship that consistently exceeds client expectations.
