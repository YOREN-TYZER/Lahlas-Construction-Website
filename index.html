About Us

Lahlas Construction is a trusted, 100% Black Economic Empowerment (BEE)-owned construction company delivering professional services across Limpopo, Gauteng, Mpumalanga, Free State, and North West. We specialize in tiling, ceilings, plumbing, painting, and full renovations, as well as electrical services.
Founded in 2024 in Kromhoek village, Limpopo, our company was born from a clear mission: to fight unemployment, reduce poverty, and drive real economic development in rural South Africa. Rooted in the principles of Batho Pele—placing people at the heart of everything we do—we are committed to excellence, integrity, professionalism, and accountability.
Led by founder and managing director Mr. Lotus Nkhwashu, we provide reliable, high-quality workmanship that exceeds client expectations. Through strategic partnerships with training institutions, business support agencies, and large enterprises, we actively create opportunities for local talent and small businesses. We are registered with CIDB, NHBRC, SARS, and other key industry bodies—ready to partner with you to build a better tomorrow.
